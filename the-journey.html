Step by Step

Awareness & Alignment
Process

The Awareness & Alignment™ Model is a thought-provoking, creative partnership to bring greater awareness of habituated programs and patterns that inhibit you from living the most joyful and liberated life possible. The starting place is a goal — but the goal is not simply to achieve it. It's to bring awareness to the motives and meanings around it. Throughout the journey you will be supported with honesty, care, non-judgment and deep respect for the expert you are in you.

What Sets Us Apart

The AWE
Difference

Most coaching drives straight toward the goal. AWExpedition starts with what's underneath it. Because achieving without awareness just keeps you running in circles — chasing outcomes that never quite bring the stillness and peace you thought reaching them would. By focusing on developing greater awareness of the patterns, beliefs, emotions, and conditioned responses shaping your experience, you can learn to move through life with more clarity, alignment, and intention. When you're no longer driven by what you think you should want, you don't just achieve — you achieve what actually matters to you.

Efficient by Design

Your time (and money) matters. We begin with a one-hour session to lay the foundation. After that, 40 minutes is all we need — enough time to surface real insight without the filler. With the AWExApp™, you drop in your focus before each session so we dig in from minute one.

Because Real Growth Happens Between Sessions — AWExApp

A 40-minute session can crack something open. What you do with it the rest of the week is where the real shift happens. The AWExApp™ keeps your momentum going — with session notes, reflection questions, experiments, guided prompts, and wins to help you stay engaged and bring awareness into everyday life.

Resources Specific to Your Journey

Through AWExpedition.com and the AWExApp™, you'll be guided toward frameworks, articles, musings, and practices that align with the specific patterns and challenges arising in your own life. You leave with a journey map that captures your growth.

A Map, Not a Dependency

Within 10 sessions, the goal is for you to know how to navigate life — to recognize your patterns, trust your knowing, and move through any terrain with or without a guide. Continued coaching is always available, but standing on your own is always the point.

You Are the Expert

This site is not called TanyeeCheungCoaching because this coaching is not about me. It's not about my degrees, my accomplishments, or my credentials (though I do have a few of those fwiw). Awareness & Alignment Coaching™ is about you. You are the authority on you — your experiences, your wisdom, what's true for you. This is your AWExpedition. My role is to ask the right questions, give you room to breathe, and get out of the way of what's already in you trying to emerge. We will use the AWE framework but the journey is all yours.

Is This Right for You?

Aligned, Addition,
or Alternative

AWExpedition isn't for everyone — and that's okay. The most important thing is that you find the right support for where you are right now. Here's an honest look at where this work tends to flourish — and where something else might serve you better.

You might be a great fit

This work tends to flourish when:
  • You sense something underneath the surface but can't quite name it
  • You're tired of chasing outcomes that don't bring what you thought they would
  • You're at a crossroads — in career, identity, relationships, or simply your relationship with yourself
  • You're curious and reflective, willing to sit with what comes up and explore it during the week
  • You're ready to loosen your grip on control and open to where the path might lead. The door doesn't have to be wide open. Just not welded shut.

A natural addition

A&A Coaching pairs well with:
  • Therapy or counseling — we're forward-focused and strengths-based, a natural complement to deeper clinical work
  • Energy work or other holistic practices
  • Executive or career coaching where the inner landscape matters as much as the outer goal

An alternative may serve better

This might not be the right fit right now if:
  • Life feels so heavy that what you need most is space to be heard and held — a therapist, counselor or energy healer may be the better fit
  • You are struggling with a medical mental health issue and are taking prescribed medication — work with your psychiatrist to determine the best course of action
  • The between-session work feels out of reach — the AWExApp™ is central to your journey and the between-session work is where the magic happens. If your schedule doesn't allow for practice in between sessions, this might not be the right season for your AWExpedition — we want you to get the most from your journey.
  • You're looking for a more directive experience — someone to assess, prescribe, and hand you a plan. That's a legitimate need, just a different kind of guide.
