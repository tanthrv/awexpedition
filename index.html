The AWE Method

The Pillars of
Living in Alignment

A

Awareness

Most of us go through life on auto-pilot. Developing Awareness enables us to understand ourselves and our relationship with the world on a much deeper level. Together we will uncover the patterns, beliefs and emotions that shape your life — fostering self-reflection and mindfulness so you can navigate challenges with clarity and insight.

W

Wonder

Once we have Awareness we can approach life's unfolding with Wonder. The AWE Method will help crystallize your Why, Open you to possibilities and try Novel approaches to Discovery through Experimentation and Reflection. Awareness and Alignment Coaching will uncover patterned and habituated beliefs and actions. Through a co-creative process, we will explore different methods and practices that foster a healthy mindset and a shift in perspective allowing you to live a more fulfilling life and become the best you.

E

Embodiment

With Awareness and Wonder, we will co-create and explore practices and tools to help you embody the perspective and insights you gain. These practices and tools will help you take the actions and create habits that reflect your aligned self. The goal is to equip you to move forward on your journey with Embodied Alignment.

About Tan — Meet Your Guide

Hi, I'm Tan —
your partner in growth

For much of my life, I believed the path was clear — achieve enough, and peace would follow. I built the career, the titles, the markers. And for a long time, the model seemed to work.

It took a collision of losses — the sudden death of a colleague, a layoff on the heels of peak achievement, a marriage unraveling, a father's illness — all within four months, to crack that equation open. What surprised me most wasn't the hardship. It was that I found more genuine peace in the rubble than I ever had in the structure.

That discovery sent me on a different kind of expedition — inward.

My path has been shaped by two powerful influences. The teachings of Michael Singer — author of The Untethered Soul — moved me so deeply, both figuratively and literally, that I left New York City and relocated to Alachua, Florida to immerse myself in his teachings, all while continuing my legal career remotely. Alongside that, I deepened my understanding of human flourishing through the study of positive psychology under Martin Seligman as part of my Applied Positive Psychology Masters degree. The intersection of Eastern contemplative practice and Western science is at the heart of how I guide this work.

My grand expedition was shaped by thirty years practicing corporate law — in the public sector, Big Law, and in-house at a Fortune 50 company. Those years gave me deep insight into the challenges the profession carries. During my legal career, I had the privilege of mentoring others navigating the same path. I was honored to be recognised for this with the Ladder Award presented by the Connecticut Bar Association – Women in the Law and Young Lawyers Sections. For reflections specific to the legal profession, visit Perspectives.

My role as your guide is to co-create a safe, non-judgmental space for you to become more aware and aligned — to bring greater awareness to your values, habits, surroundings, and goals, uncover patterns standing in your way, and explore practices and tools to help your life come into alignment with your true self.

I believe deeply that you are the expert in YOU — capable, whole, and deserving of a full and authentic life. I would be honored to be a part of your journey.

How It Works

Awareness & Alignment
Process

The Awareness & Alignment™ Model is a thought-provoking, creative partnership to bring greater awareness of habituated programs and patterns that inhibit you from living the most joyful and liberated life possible. The starting place is a goal — but the goal is not simply to achieve it. It's to bring awareness to the motives and meanings around it. In so doing, you will likely discover what is holding you back, and we will co-creatively explore ways to overcome those blockers — or you might find goals that better align with your true self. Throughout the journey you will be supported with honesty, care, non-judgment and deep respect for the expert you are in you.

01

Free Consultation

02
